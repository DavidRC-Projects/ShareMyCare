Born from a Critical Need

ShareMyCare was created after a life-threatening experience where a paramedic's assessment was completely wrong, and medical records were inaccessible even with proper consent. As a healthcare professional, intervention was required to ensure proper care - a decision that ultimately saved a life.

This highlighted a fundamental gap: in emergency situations, healthcare providers need immediate access to complete medical histories to make accurate assessments. Patients and families must be able to share critical health information when it matters most.
