A Simple Truth: Better Access Means Better Care

ShareMyCare was built to solve a problem that should have been solved long ago:

  1. Patients should be able to securely carry their full medical picture with them.
  2. Clinicians should be able to access it instantly—with the patient's permission—anytime, anywhere.
  3. No more missing history, no more incorrect assumptions, no more preventable harm.

This platform exists because I never want another family to experience what mine did.

And because every healthcare professional deserves the tools to make the best, safest, and fastest decisions possible.

For Patients

  • Control over your data
  • Easy sharing with clinicians
  • Share with family

For Clinicians

  • Complete patient history
  • Reduced history-taking time
  • All medications listed
  • Document intelligence for quick and secure note storage

For Emergency Responders

  • Instant access to critical info
  • Complete medication lists
  • Allergy information
  • Faster decision-making
