Introduction

ShareMyCare ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, and contact information
  • Date of birth and demographic information
  • Health information you choose to enter (medications, conditions, allergies, assessments)
  • Account credentials and authentication information

Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information about your use of the platform:

  • Device information and browser type
  • IP address and location data
  • Usage patterns and interactions with the platform

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the ShareMyCare platform
  • Process your requests and transactions
  • Enable sharing of your health information with authorized clinicians
  • Improve our services and user experience
  • Send you important updates and notifications
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information:

  • With your explicit consent: When you grant access to specific clinicians
  • For legal compliance: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • With service providers: Trusted third parties who assist in operating our platform (under strict confidentiality agreements)

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data
  • Control who has access to your health information
  • Revoke access granted to clinicians at any time
  • Export your data

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including encryption, secure authentication, and regular security audits. For more details, see our Security page.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
