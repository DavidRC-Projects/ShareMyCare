Common Questions

How do I share my records with a clinician?

Go to your dashboard, click "Share with Clinicians", and either enter a practitioner code or invite them via email.

Can I revoke access to my records?

Yes, you can revoke access at any time from your dashboard under "Share with Clinicians".

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and security measures. See our Security page for more details.

How do I update my profile information?

Go to your dashboard and click "Edit" on the Personal Information card to update your details.
