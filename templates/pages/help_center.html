Getting Started

Creating Your Account

To get started with ShareMyCare:

  1. Click "Get Started" on the homepage
  2. Fill in your registration details
  3. Verify your email address
  4. Start adding your health information

Adding Health Information

You can add various types of health information:

  • Medications: Current and past medications with dosages
  • Conditions: Medical conditions and diagnoses
  • Allergies: Known allergies and reactions
  • Assessments: Health assessments and notes
  • Work History: Current and previous employment

Sharing with Clinicians

Using Practitioner Codes

To share your records using a practitioner code:

  1. Go to your dashboard
  2. Click "Share with Clinicians"
  3. Enter the practitioner code provided by your clinician
  4. Select the access level you want to grant
  5. Confirm the sharing

Inviting Clinicians by Email

You can also invite clinicians via email:

  1. Go to "Share with Clinicians" in your dashboard
  2. Click "Invite +"
  3. Enter the clinician's email address
  4. Select access level and permissions
  5. Send the invitation

For Healthcare Professionals

Creating a Clinician Account

Healthcare professionals can create an account to access patient records:

  1. Click "Healthcare Professional login" on the homepage
  2. Fill in your professional details
  3. Complete your profile with registration information
  4. Start connecting with patients

Creating Assessments

Clinicians can create assessments for their patients:

  • Use the quick upload feature to photograph notes
  • Fill in detailed assessment forms
  • Add objective measures and findings
  • Extract findings automatically from note images
